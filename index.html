    Computer Programming

    Under Tech-Voc Track ICT Strand Which Leads to BS Computer Science or BS Entertainment Multimedia Computing

    The Programming track of CIIT allows its students to experience real-world programming firsthand while in Senior High School. The track allows students to immerse themselves in an industry ready curriculum which focuses on developing their knowledge and skills in the current methods of software development and design. Even in the online setting, students will still be able to experience this on-hands learning effectively as teachers will share their expertise in the area whilst demonstrating it live to the class for efficient understanding.


    What sets our Computer Programming apart from others?

    Adaptation of the Code.org curriculum
    CODE.ORG is a US-based organization that makes computer science and programming skills more accessible to students and other minorities worldwide. The curriculum is developed by engineers in Google, Facebook, Microsoft, Twitter, and Amazon.com. CIIT Philippines is the first Senior High School in the country to adopt this curriculum

    Preparation for Philippine Programmers Rating (PPR)
    Learn to code the right way and earn the ability to make speedy logical decisions and creative processes through the 560-hour extensive computer laboratory-based training geared towards preparing our students to take the Philippine Programmers Rating (PPR). The PPR is an examination recognized by numerous members of the Philippine Software Industry Association and is the first automated assessment tool aligned with the needs of the Philippine software industry. The examination was developed to identify entry-level programming talent in the country.
